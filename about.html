About the Conference

ICTYVM 2025 – International Conference on Traditional Systems of Healing: Bridging Yoga, Varma, and Modern Science – is a significant event that explores the integration of ancient healing practices with modern science.

Yoga involves physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation, and has shown great potential in managing stress, chronic pain, and anxiety. Varma, a traditional healing system that uses pressure points, complements these practices.

Why ICTYVM 2025?

  • Connect traditional healing knowledge with scientific innovations
  • Learn from global research experts
  • Explore the role of Yoga and Varma in modern healthcare
  • Showcase your research and gain recognition

This conference provides a unique platform for researchers, scholars, and practitioners to share insights, present papers, and build collaborations in integrative health.
