Call for Papers

We invite researchers, scholars, and practitioners to contribute to the ICTYVM 2025 conference through paper submissions. Share your innovative research, insights, and perspectives across traditional systems of healing.

Conference Themes

  • Entrepreneurial Opportunities in Yoga, Varma, and Healing Sciences
  • Yoga, Media, and Mass Communication in the Digital Era
  • Varma Therapy: Ancient Techniques for Modern Ailments
  • Healing Medicine and Siddha-Based Varma Applications
  • Integrative Approaches in Varma, Yoga Therapy, and Evidence-based Medicine
  • Case Studies on Varma and Yoga Therapy in Clinical and Community Settings
  • Philosophical and Scientific Foundations of Traditional and Modern Yoga
  • Neurological and Cognitive Benefits of Yoga and Meditation
  • Yoga for Holistic Living and Lifestyle Transformation
  • Spiritual Tourism and Wellness Journeys through Yoga
  • Value-based Education and Integrative Yoga Models in Academia

Conference Date & Venue

Date: 15th August 2025
Venue: International Youth Centre (IYC), Jalan Yaacob Latif, Bandar Tun Razak, 56000 Cheras Kuala Lumpur

We look forward to your valuable submissions and active participation!
